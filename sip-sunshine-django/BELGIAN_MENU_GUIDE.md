## Belgian Cuisine Highlights

### What Makes This Menu Belgian?

1. **Mussels & Fries** - Belgium's most famous dish
   - Fresh North Sea mussels
   - Perfectly crispy Belgian fries with traditional sauces
   - Available September-April (peak season)

2. **Carbonnade Flamande** - Traditional Flemish Beef Stew
   - Beef braised in Trappist beer
   - Served with Belgian fries
   - Protected heritage status from Flanders

3. **Waterzooi** - Creamy Vegetable & Meat Stew
   - Traditional Ghent specialty
   - Originated in Flanders region
   - Perfect winter comfort food

4. **Belgian Waffles** - World Famous Desserts
   - Liege Waffles: Street food with pearl sugar, warm & dense
   - Brussels Waffles: Lighter, crispier, served with toppings
   - Made with authentic Belgian chocolate

5. **Belgian Chocolate** - Godiva & Local Artisans
   - Finest chocolate in the world
   - Used in mousse and tarts
   - Pralines are Belgium's signature treat

6. **Belgian Beer** - UNESCO Protected Heritage
   - Trappist beers: Brewed by monks, unique recipes
   - Lambic beers: Spontaneously fermented in Brussels
   - Dubbel/Tripel: Strong, complex flavors
   - 12 recognized Trappist breweries worldwide
